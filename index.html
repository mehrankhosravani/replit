HOW TO USE QORE.

From idea to output —
in just three simple steps.

  • STEP 01

    Pick a goal

    Choose the type of content you want to create: a tagline, description, image, video idea, and more.

  • STEP 02

    Add a prompt

    Enter keywords, context, or any starting idea. Upload images or videos to guide the result.

  • STEP 03

    Get results

    Qore returns multiple outputs. Choose your favorite, refine it, or keep exploring more options.

[ FAQS ]

ANSWERS TO YOUR COMMON QUESTIONS.

Got questions?
We've got answers.

What can I use Qore for?

You can generate taglines, short descriptions, ideas for visuals, social posts, and more in seconds.

Is Qore free to use?

Qore offers a free tier with limited generations. Paid plans unlock more features and outputs.

Can I use Qore for commercial projects?

Yes — all generated content can be used for both personal and commercial purposes.

How accurate are the outputs?

Results are context-aware and high quality, but we recommend reviewing and refining for best use.

What if I don’t like the result?

You can regenerate as many times as needed, or edit outputs directly to suit your needs.
